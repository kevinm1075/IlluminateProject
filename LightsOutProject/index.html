What is iLLuMiNaTe?

iLLuMiNaTe is a remake of the 1997 electronic game "Lights Out", where the player's goal was to turn off all the lit tiles on the game board. The only difference here, is instead of turning all the lights off, we now aim to turn them on! However unlike the the old game, there is a simple AI which can solve the board for the player if they cannot solve it themselves. If at any point you become stuck, simply click the solve button and the sequence of moves required to solve the puzzle will be presented to you. For more information on how the puzzles are solved, please naviagte to the 'about' section of the site.
